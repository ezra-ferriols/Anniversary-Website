To My Love ❤️

Happy anniversary, my Love! This is just a simple gift to wander around our memories from the past year. You know, throughout the year, there’s only one thing that has never left me: how grateful I am to have you. Every day and night, you’ve shown unwavering love, support, patience, and understanding. Not a single day have you shown pure anger; you’ve always been the Monel I love when it comes to me.

Ika ko nga lagi na napakaswerte mo sa akin, but the truth is I’m really the lucky one to have you. You’ve always been there, showing me how lucky I am to be your boyfriend. No matter what others don’t see in you, I’m so lucky that I get to see that side of you. Whether you are crying, emotional, a disaster, happy, or joyful, I’ll always appreciate what I see in you.

As we grow old together, noticing every change, getting frustrated at everything, and still sharing that little smile even on our bad days, we will have our own conflicts and arguments, and countless apologies. Yet I can’t seem to unlove or leave you, and if I did, I don’t think I would ever forgive myself.

You’re extraordinary, but not in a bad way; that’s always something I say about you. You’re not like others: the way you are kind, how passionate you are, and even how patient you are. God, there are so many adjectives to describe what a wonderful person you are. There’s just something about you that makes me fall in love with you more each day. Who knows?

My love, thank you for everything you have given me. I may have a lot of broken promises, but I promise you that I will never leave you, and you will always have me.

I have loved you, I love you, and I will always love you, forever and ever.
